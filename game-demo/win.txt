Why do we play games? Life is so fragile, a successful virus clinging to a speck of mud, suspended in endless nothing.
Our lives are so brief and unimportant. The cosmos cares nothing for us.
Everything in the world displeases me. Above all, my displeasure in everything displeases me.
Destroy or be destroyed. There is no middle way.
All good things were formerly bad things. Every original evil has turned into an original virtue.
I may never win again or at anything else.
